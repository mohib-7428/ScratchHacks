# Scratch Workspace Scripting Help
A collection of 45 scripts for the Scratch (Blockly) editor to customize, glitch, and have fun with your workflow.

---
## How to Use These Scripts
1. Open any project in the [Scratch Editor](https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/editor/).
2. Press **F12** (or `Right-Click > Inspect`) on your browser.
3. Click the **Console** tab in the panel that appears.
4. Copy a script from this file, paste it into the console, and press **Enter**.
5. **Note:** Most visual changes are temporary. Simply **refresh the page** to return Scratch to its normal state.
